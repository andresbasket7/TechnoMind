TechnoMind

Welcome to our magazine. The name comes from the mix between technology and human reasoning, from how we can develop our behavior from the technological influence that is applied to us every day without perceiving it.

Here you can find articles related to persuasive technology and cognitive science. As the design, development and evaluation of interactive technologies can influence the behavior of the person through techniques of persuasion and social influence.
